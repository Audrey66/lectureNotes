    ∨ - look at two sides, can you prove one of them by themselves, use OrI1 OrI2

        see if you have an or statement, if so, try OrE to build conclusion

    → - use ImplyI subproof. assume left side, try and get right side

    ¬(something) - use NegI subproof. assume "something", try and get F (contradiction)

        do you have or statement as claim? try OrE to get atom in both cases
